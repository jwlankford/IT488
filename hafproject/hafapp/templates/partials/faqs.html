What services do you offer?

We offer personalized health and fitness consulting services, including workout plans, nutritional guidance, and progress tracking.

What are your pricing plans?

We offer three pricing plans: Basic at $99/month, Premium at $149/month, and Elite at $199/month. Each plan offers different levels of service to cater to your specific needs.

How often will I have check-ins?

The frequency of check-ins depends on your chosen plan. The Basic Plan includes monthly progress tracking, the Premium Plan offers bi-weekly check-ins, and the Elite Plan provides weekly one-on-one sessions.

Can I switch plans?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Please contact our support team for assistance.

What is included in the nutritional guidance?

Our nutritional guidance includes personalized meal planning based on your dietary preferences and fitness goals.

What if I have specific dietary restrictions or allergies?

Our consultants are trained to accommodate specific dietary needs. Please inform us of any restrictions or allergies during your initial assessment.

Do I need any special equipment for the workout plans?

Most of our workout plans can be done with minimal equipment. If any special equipment is needed, it will be clearly stated in the plan.

What if I have a pre-existing medical condition?

Please consult with your healthcare provider before starting any new fitness program. We can adjust your plan according to any restrictions or recommendations from your healthcare provider.
